Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Embo TV?

Embo TV is an Over-The-Top (OTT) video streaming platform that allows users to watch a wide range of content, including movies, TV shows, and original programming, on demand.

2. How can I access Embo TV?

You can access Embo TV through our website, Smart TV or by downloading our mobile app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Simply create an account, and you'll be able to start streaming content right away.

3. What kind of content does Embo TV offer?

Embo TV offers a diverse selection of content, including African movies, TV shows, documentaries, and original programming. We regularly update our library to ensure that there's always something new and exciting to watch.

4. Can I watch Embo TV on multiple devices?

You can login two devices on standard plan and watch Embo TV one each device at a time, Simply log in and watch. E-Business gives you the option of having as many devices login on one account. Means you can have minimum of 10 devices up to a 1000 devices under one account with multiple login in one location. This plan is best for hospitality industry, hotels, companies and airline with on-board viewing including smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, and computers. Simply log in to your account and choose a plan that best suits your need.

5. Is there a subscription fee for Embo TV?

Yes, Embo TV offers both Standard plan, E-Business and E-Box for your premium subscription options. With a premium subscription, you'll have access to additional content and features, such as streaming and offline downloads. All Embo TV plan are all Ad free.

6. Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription to Embo TV at any time. Simply log in to your account and navigate to the subscription settings to manage your membership.
